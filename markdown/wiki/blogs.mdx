- [Mike Coutermash](https://mikecoutermarsh.com/): This is the personal blog of Mike Coutermash, a software engineer working for GitHub.
- [Antirez](http://antirez.com/latest/0): The blog of the developer of Redis.
- [Aral Balkan](https://ar.al): I’m a cyborg rights activist, designer, and developer at Small Technology Foundation. Drop me a line for public speaking or media inquiries.
- [Chris Krycho](https://v5.chriskrycho.com): I’m a software engineer by trade; a writer, podcaster, and occasional composer by hobby; and a theologian by vocation.
- [Felix Rieseberg](https://felixrieseberg.com): I love building apps and empowering people.
- [NSHipster](https://nshipster.com): NSHipster is a journal of the overlooked bits in Objective-C, Swift, and Cocoa. Updated weekly.
- [Ole Begemann](https://oleb.net/blog/): I build iOS and Mac apps, mostly as a contractor, and I write about software development. I’m co-author of a book titled Advanced Swift.
- [Zach Holman](https://zachholman.com): I’m a developer and startup advisor living in San Francisco. I like it here because it’s usually pretty warm but not too warm where I worry about wearing shorts and junk.
- [Cocoa with love](https://www.cocoawithlove.com): I work as a software developer and consultant based in Melbourne, Australia; both independently and through my streaming media technologies company Zqueue.
- [Artsy engineering blog](https://artsy.github.io)
